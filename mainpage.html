Accident and Emergency

The Accident & Emergency Department caters to patients sustaining any form or severity of primary injury, or suffering from any medical emergency, or sudden severe illness. Also life threating conditions are treated in the A & E department viz. unconsciousness, persistent chest pain, breathing difficulties, bleeding, burns or scars, allergic reactions, etc.

3

ENT Department

The department of Ear Nose Throat has over the year's evolved into one of the most advanced and sophisticated centers of Jamshedpur. It works on the principle of “to provide medical facilities of the highest standard at an affordable cost to all segments of society & to do charitable work, to promote academic excellence, to share professional knowledge with all colleagues”.

4

Department of Surgery

The Department of General Surgery was among the first few departments started at TATA Motors Hospital in 1969, making it one of the oldest dedicated surgical units in the region. The Department provides the most up-to-date quality surgical care to our patients including daily OPDs, round the clock emergency services, facility for Surgical ICU, Cabins and state-of-art Male and Female Surgical wards with hi-tech Modular Operation Theatres, one of which is dedicated for Minimal Access surgery. With the acquisition of latest surgical equipments over time and a well trained surgical team, we provide the widest possible coverage for all types of surgical ailments including Polytrauma, Urology, Onco-surgery, Minimal access surgery and Therapeutic endoscopic services
